Vegetables

They are a crucial part of a balanced diet, providing essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber. While some, like tomatoes, are botanically classified as fruits, they are commonly used as vegetables in culinary contexts.

Many flowers are considered beautiful, with some of the most popular being roses, orchids, tulips, and dahlias. These flowers are admired for their vibrant colors, intricate petal arrangements, and delicate fragrances.

Fruits

Fruits are the mature ovaries of flowering plants, typically containing seeds and often fleshy and sweet, that are eaten as food. Botanically, they are the means by which angiosperms (flowering plants) disperse their seeds. Common examples include apples, bananas, grapes, oranges, and strawberries
